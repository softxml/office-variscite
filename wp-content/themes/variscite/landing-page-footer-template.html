About Variscite

For almost two decades, Variscite is developing, producing, and manufacturing a powerful range of System on Modules, consistently setting market benchmarks in terms of speed and innovation. The company’s System on Module (SoM) portfolio is based on ARM technologies (ARM Cortex-A72, Cortex-A35, Cortex-A8, Cortex-A9, Cortex-A7, and ARM Cortex-A53, Cortex-A55) embedded inside leading SoC solutions.

In recent times, the company is focusing on developing a wide range of Computer on Modules (CoM) based on NXP’s i.MX9, i.MX8, i.MX6 and i.MX7 applications processors series and Texas Instruments AM625:

  • NXP i.MX 9 series – i.MX93, i.MX91, i.MX95
  • NXP i.MX 8 series – i.MX8 QuadMax / QuadPlus, iMX 8X, i.MX 8M, i.MX 8M Mini, i.MX 8M Nano, i.MX 8M Plus
  • NXP i.MX 6 series – iMX 6, i.MX 6UL, i.MX 6ULL, i.MX 6ULZ
  • NXP i.MX 7 series – iMX7
  • TI AM625

All Variscite production is performed at fully ISO 13485 compliant facilities, satisfying international customers and regulatory requirements for a broad range of industries including medical devices and related services. The company’s production facilities are equipped with the most advanced SMT machines that ensure punctual deliveries and high-quality products.

NXP i.MX8 System on Module

The iMX 8 System on Module series runs on NXP’s iMX8 processors up to 6 cores ARM Cortex architectures, including Cortex-A72, Cortex-A53, and Cortex-A35, combined with Cortex-M4, Cortex-M7 and Cortex-4x real-time coprocessors.

Variscite’s portfolio provides the maximum spectrum of performance, cost, and scalability with full support in all NXP’s iMX8 processors; from power-efficiency and cost-sensitive solutions based on iMX8 Nano and iMX8M Mini platforms, through the high-end multimedia applications performance of the iMX 8M and iMX8M Plus platforms, up to iMX8X and iMX 8 platforms with advanced processing power, a wide range of connectivity and impressive multimedia performance.
